Bionic Materials and Systems Research

Our Mission

We conduct interdisciplinary research interfacing:

  • Biomedical Science
  • Mechanical and Materials Sciences & Engineering
  • Electronics & Wireless Communication Engineering
  • Bionanotechnology

Direct multidimensional integration of functional, electronic and mechanical components with viable biological systems allows for the creation of bionic systems and devices possessing unique and advanced capabilities and thus opens up tremendous opportunities across a broad array of disciplines in science and engineering. These range from the realm of direct interfacing of functional electronic and mechanical elements with pre-grown, mature biological tissue and systems, to the development of seamlessly merged, chimeric bionic systems possessing advanced functionalities.
